Ham Sandwich

Description

This is a simple ham sandwich

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Deli Ham
  • Swiss or American cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Empty stomach

Steps

  1. Get a plate
  2. Get two slices of white bread
  3. Put two slices of ham on one piece of bread
  4. Put a liberal amount of ketchup on top of ham
  5. Put a liberal amount of mayo on top of the ham and ketchup
  6. Devour your masterpiece
