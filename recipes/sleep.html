Sleep Better

Description

Guide to better sleep

Ingredients

  • Dead phone battery
  • Cool body temperature
  • Dark room
  • Cozy bed

Steps

  1. Try not to shower less than two hours before sleep
  2. Make sure to stop caffeine intake eight hours prior to sleep
  3. Limit phone usage one hour before sleep
  4. Do not use a computer or television one hour before sleep
  5. Try to create a dark room by drawing curtains or closing all doors
  6. Set AC / Heater to optimal temp
  7. Find a comfortable sleeping position
  8. Wear an eye mask or ear plugs if desired
  9. Have sweet dreams
