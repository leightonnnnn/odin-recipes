Candied Yams

Description

This is how to make candied yams

Ingredients

  • 3 Yams
  • Brown sugar
  • Heat

Steps

  1. Peel outer skin off yams
  2. Cut yams into semi-large chunks
  3. Heat pot on low
  4. Put yams into pot
  5. Pour two cups of brown sugar onto yams
  6. Put lid on pot
  7. Cook yams at a low heat for 30 minutes
  8. Eat yams when they are soft and glowing
